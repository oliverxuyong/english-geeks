I want to build an interactive webpage.
The page is designed for learning a short English passage.
Each sentence will be separated and studied one by one.
Users can practice vocabulary before they listen to the full audio.
The listening step helps them hear natural pronunciation and rhythm.
Practice cards show Chinese translation and IPA above each word.
Three difficulty tabs control how many words are blanked out.
Swipe up or down to move between sentences on a touch screen.
Swipe left or right to switch between beginner and advanced levels.
The Speak button uses speech recognition until you tap Stop.
Matched words turn green as you read, even when they were blanked.
After practice, play the full passage again to feel your progress.
